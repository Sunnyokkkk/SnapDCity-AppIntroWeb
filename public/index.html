Snap D City

A free simple-to-use, Android App that helps disabled residents who face difficulties in accessing public facilities in the Melbourne CBD. It allows users to search for the location of parking spots and toilets as well as how to report a misuse.

You can open an online emulator by clicking the GET STARTED button below
